ECO IS THE PLACE

What is ECO?

ECO, Environment, Community and Organisation focuses on solving local and global problems with the promise of enabling young innovators and business owners to make an impact

Anyone Can Share

You dont have to sign up to submit, if you see an issue which effects the Environment, Community or Organisation in your local or global area, report it on the platform.

Structure

The Platform lives and breathes via local as well as global problems being reported and collected by the database. These get turned into projects by Leaders who collaborate with Partners and assign tasks to Ambassadors.

Friendly Support

Our freindly network of Ambassadors, Leaders and Partners are here on the platform to support you in collborating as well as creating projects. Finding like minded people just got a whole lot easier.

The Best Interface

Our interface is itergrated with the most modern and secure technology to enable everyone to feel safe while their data is secure.

Transparent System

The system we create is to enable everyone to have access to technology and equal opportunity grow as well develop their ambition.

Gamification

Here at ECO we work with many companies who strive for future ways to make working environemts more producitive and sustainable.
